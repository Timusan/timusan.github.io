Okinawa high school graduation

← back

Foobar At the beginning of March the Japanese schoolyear ends, bringing about many happy, graduating students. In Okinawa, graduating from high school brings an extra, colorful dimension. Parents, teachers and fellow students get to give presents to their children, pupils and friends, making for a very different sight then the more sterile graduation ceremony on the mainland of Japan.
